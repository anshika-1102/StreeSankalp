Empowering Women, Enhancing Safety.

Our mission-driven platform is dedicated to creating a safer and more empowering world for women. By leveraging advanced technologies and community-driven initiatives, we aim to foster environments where women feel safe, supported, and valued. We believe in the power of technology, community, and information to drive real change, ensuring that every woman has the tools and resources needed to thrive with confidence and resilience.

Protecting Today, Empowering Tomorrow.

To protect, empower, and inspire women by providing innovative safety solutions and resources that enhance personal security, build awareness, and foster a global community dedicated to supporting women’s rights and well-being. Through proactive safety analytics, real-time monitoring, and community engagement, we aim to make every space a safe space for women.

A Safe World for Every Woman, Everywhere.

A world where every woman feels safe, empowered, and free to pursue her dreams without fear or limitations. Our vision is to create a global impact, breaking down societal barriers and ensuring that every woman has access to the tools, support, and opportunities to realize her full potential in a world that values her safety and empowerment.

What we do

How We Make a Difference !!!

Anonymous Reporting

Enables users to report incidents or concerns without revealing their identity, ensuring privacy and encouraging more women to come forward without fear.
Movement Monitoring

Tracks user's movements in real-time, providing alerts to selected contacts if unusual activity is detected, enhancing safety during travel or in unfamiliar areas.
Chatbot Assistance

Offers instant support and information via an AI-powered chatbot, guiding users through safety tips, emergency contacts, and support resources tailored to their needs.
Fake Video Calling

Allows users to simulate a live video call, deterring potential threats by creating the impression of being engaged and supervised, especially useful in uncomfortable situations.

Late 19th - Early 20th Century

The Suffragette Movement

Fought for women's right to vote, with key figures like Susan B. Anthony, Elizabeth Cady Stanton, and Emmeline Pankhurst.

1960s-1980s

The Women's Liberation Movement

Advocated for equality in all aspects of life, including reproductive rights, workplace equality, and sexual liberation.

2017-Present

The #MeToo Movement

A social movement against sexual harassment and assault, empowering women to speak out about their experiences.

Multiple Waves, 19th Century-Present

The Feminist Movement

Advocated for equal rights for women in various areas, including legal rights, reproductive rights, and ending gender discrimination.

2017-Present

The Women's March

A global movement advocating for women's rights, including issues such as reproductive rights, LGBTQIA+ rights, and racial justice.

Late 20th Century-Present

Girls' Education Movement

Focused on advocating for education for girls around the world, ensuring they have equal access to schooling and educational resources.

1960s-Present

Reproductive Rights Movement

Focuses on advocating for women's rights to make decisions about their reproductive health, including birth control, abortion, and maternity leave

Ongoing

The International Day for the Elimination of Violence Against Women (Orange Day)

A global campaign to raise awareness about violence against women and girls, advocating for legal reforms and social changes to protect them.

2014-Present

The HeForShe Campaign

A solidarity movement initiated by UN Women to engage men and boys in achieving gender equality, emphasizing that gender equality benefits all.

2018-Present

Time's Up Movement

A movement to combat workplace sexual harassment and gender inequality, particularly in the entertainment industry, with legal advocacy and a fund to support survivors.

2013-Present

#BlackLivesMatter and Womens Rights

A movement that highlights the intersectionality of race and gender, focusing on issues of police brutality, racial inequality, and the unique struggles faced by Black women/

2014-Present

The #BringBackOurGirls Movement

Focused on the kidnapping of 276 schoolgirls by Boko Haram in Nigeria, this movement advocates for the safe return of abducted girls and women globally.
