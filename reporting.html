What We Do to Protect People

What is Gender-Based Violence?
Gender-based violence encompasses harmful acts directed at individuals based on their gender, often resulting in physical, psychological, or economic harm. It is rooted in gender inequality, power imbalances, and social norms that support discriminatory practices.
What is Domestic Violence?
Domestic violence refers to abusive behavior by one partner toward another in a household or intimate relationship. It can include physical, emotional, or psychological abuse, aiming to control or dominate the victim.
What is Sexual Abuse?
Sexual abuse involves unwanted or coerced sexual acts or contact imposed on an individual, violating their autonomy and dignity. This can include assault, exploitation, or harassment and can leave lasting physical and emotional harm.
What is Eve Teasing ?
Eve teasing is a form of sexual harassment, typically involving unwelcome comments, gestures, or physical advances directed at women in public spaces. It undermines safety and reinforces gender-based discrimination.
